The Outsider is a gripping drama that delves into the life of John Walker, a war veteran who struggles to reintegrate into society after returning home from his final tour in Afghanistan. Set in a small American town, the film explores themes of isolation, redemption, and the challenges of finding one's place in a world that has moved on without them.

John Walker (Main Protagonist): A war veteran struggling with PTSD and reintegration into civilian life.

Sarah Walker (John's Wife): A resilient woman trying to keep her family together despite the challenges.

Emma Walker (John's Daughter): A young girl caught between her love for her father and the fear of his unpredictable behavior.
